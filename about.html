My Story

Welcome to my kitchen! I'm Sameena, and cooking has been my passion for as long as I can remember. Growing up in a North Indian household, I was surrounded by the aromatic spices and comforting flavors that define our cuisine.

What started as helping my mother in the kitchen has evolved into a journey of preserving and sharing traditional recipes with a modern twist. I believe that good food doesn't have to be complicated ‚Äì it just needs love, the right ingredients, and clear instructions.

Through my Instagram Reels and this website, I share easy-to-follow recipes that fit into your daily routine. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced cook, my goal is to make North Indian cooking accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
